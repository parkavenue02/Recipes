Taco

Description

Quick and easy taco filling for the whole family to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 package dry taco seasoning mix
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 lb skinless, boneless chicken breast

Steps

  1. Mix chicken broth and taco seasoning in a bowl
  2. Place chicken breast in a slow cooker and pour chicken broth mixture over chicken
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours
  4. Shred chicken and serve!
